(03) Barracão Skateshop
Barracão Skateshop, 2025
TypeBranding / Graphic Design
ClientBarracão Skateshop
Year2025
RoleBrand Identity & Graphic Design
ToolsIllustrator, Photoshop
New visual identity for a skate shop built by skaters, for skaters.

Barracão isn't just a skateshop — it's a space where skating, art and culture converge, created and managed 100% by skaters. The brief was to develop a logo and concept arts that reflected this authentic identity, moving away from generic action-sports aesthetics.

The identity centers on the idea that Barracão exists to celebrate the true essence of skating. The blue and white palette references classic skate graphics with a contemporary edge. "Por skatistas, para skatistas" anchors all brand messaging.

(04) Dissidente Skate Video
Dissidente Skate Video, 2025
TypeGraphic Design
ClientBarracão & Nogenta
DirectorHumberto P. & VT Silva
Year2025
RoleGraphic Design
ToolsPhotoshop, Illustrator
Graphics and video premiere art for the "Dissidente" skate video.

Dissidente is a skate video produced by Humberto P. and VT Silva, presented by Barracão & Nogenta. The project needed a full visual identity for the video premiere — posters, digital assets, and graphics that captured the raw, underground energy of the skate scene in Rio.

The visual language was built around a custom hand-style typography — aggressive, dripping letterforms that echo graffiti and street culture. The contrast between the heavy type and photographic backgrounds creates tension without losing the underground roots of the project.
